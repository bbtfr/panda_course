Welcome to PANDA Course 2014

PANDA course is jointly organized by School of Computer Science and Technology, Shandong University, China, Open Security Research and Sinochip Semiconductors. PANDA aims at providing a platform for security design engineers, analysts and researchers to exchange ideas and discuss recent advances in the field of cryptographic circuits.
